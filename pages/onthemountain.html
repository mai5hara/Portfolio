On The Mountain

PROJECTUI/UX Design, Developent

USE LANGUAGE, TOOLSSketch / Figma / Java / Android Studio

CONCEPTThis app is a recipe app for cooking in the mountains. My hobby is mountain climbing, but every time I go to the mountains, I always care about the weight of food and search for foods that help me get calories efficiently. I thought it would be useful if there was an app that suggested dishes based on the difficulty level of the mountain and favorite ingredients, so I decided to create this app. This app has not only a recipe list but also mountain information and dish sharing features.
